Home

Welcome to my final project! This is a brief demo of the library Matter.js, which is a simple 2D physics engine.

Navigation

To navigate through the functions of my site, click the drop-down menu at the top right of the screen.

Playground

Playground will be initialized with a few basic objects in the environment. Use the menu
on the top of the screen to adjust the parameters of the object you would like to add to the environment,
then click the 'Create Object' button to drop it into the environment.

Plinko

A classic game of Plinko. Click 'Drop Ball' to drop a ball into the Plinko environment.

Angry Winged Animals

A 100% original idea that has absolutely no relation to the game 'Angry Birds'. Click and drag
the ball on the left side of the screen and release to shoot the ball.
